The Default Community

The Official APK provided above is pre-configured with a shared cloud backend. While your communications are E2E encrypted, your data resides on the main Mono infrastructure. This is ideal for public testing and open community participation.

  • • Optimized for immediate hierarchical thread exploration
  • • Shared with the global Mono research community
  • • Zero-configuration required for instant messaging
Private Deployments

For absolute independence, we recommend building your own binary. To create a private community with friends, ensure all participants use an APK compiled with your unique SUPABASE_URL and ANON_KEY. This ensures total data isolation from the default instance.

  • • Total isolation from the shared community database
  • • User-controlled storage and synchronization policy
  • • Ideal for private organizations and close-knit groups
