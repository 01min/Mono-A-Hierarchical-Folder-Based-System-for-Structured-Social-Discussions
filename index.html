E2E Encrypted

Every Space you join is protected by industry-standard encryption. Only members of the space can decrypt and read the threads.

Nested Spaces

Organize your thoughts with hierarchical spaces. Create a parent space and drop specialized sub-folders to keep discussions focused.

Local First

Your "General" space never touches the cloud. Everything is stored on your device using a high-performance local database.

Built on Sovereignty.

Mono isn't just an app; it's a statement. By using Supabase for cloud sync and local SQLite for storage, we ensure that you own your identity and your data.

The Privacy Promise

We abolished the "Like" button to remove social pressure. We added hierarchical folders to encourage deep thinking. Mono is about the quality of the discussion, not the quantity of the noise.
